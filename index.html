Disaster Preparation

Crises and emergency situations can happen at any time and are often unexpected. Wilderness Survival teaches the strategies and mindset required to prevail over challenging circumstances. The specific techniques described below provide essential information for survival without modern technology; however, the principles of wilderness survival encourage preparedness and calm thinking in any high-stress situation.

There are three essential requirements for a human to survive, and one must carefully consider which they should prioritize when time is not a luxury.

  1. Shelter
  2. Fire
  3. Water
